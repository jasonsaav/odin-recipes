Churros

Description

A churro is a cinnamon and sugar topped fried pastry dough

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 quarts oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Combine water, 2 Â½ tablespoons sugar, salt, and 2 tablespoons vegetable oil in a small saucepan and place over medium heat. Bring to a boil and remove from the heat
  2. Stir in flour until mixture forms a ball
  3. Heat oil for frying in a deep fryer or deep pot to 375 degrees F. Transfer dough to a sturdy pastry bag fitted with a medium star tip
  4. Carefully pipe a few 5- to 6-inch strips of dough into the hot oil; work in batches so you don't crowd the fryer
  5. Cook until golden; use a spider or slotted spoon to transfer churros to paper towels to drain.
  6. Combine 1/2 cup sugar and cinnamon. Roll drained churros in cinnamon and sugar mixture
