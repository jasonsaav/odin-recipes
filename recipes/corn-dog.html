Corn Dog

Description

Corn dogs are hot dogs dipped in a cornmeal batter, and then fried until golden

Ingredients

  • 1 packages beef frankfurters
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 quart vegetable oil
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup flour
  • wooden skewers

Steps

  1. Whisk cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl; stir in milk and egg to make a batter
  2. Heat oil in a large saucepan to 375 degrees F
  3. Insert wooden skewers into frankfurters and pat them dry
  4. Roll frankfurters in batter until well coated
  5. Fry corn dogs until golden brown, about 3-5 minutes
  6. Drain on paper towels
