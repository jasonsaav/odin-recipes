Corn Dog

Description

Corn dogs are hot dogs dipped in a cornmeal batter, and then fried until golden

Ingredients

  • 1 packages beef frankfurters
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 quart vegetable oil
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup flour
  • wooden skewers
