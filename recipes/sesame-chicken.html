Sesame Chicken

Description

Sesame Chicken is a staple of Chinese-American cuisine, famous for its perfect balance of crunchy, sweet, and savory flavors

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Whisk cornstarch, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl; add chicken and toss to coat
  2. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken in batches, turning it from time to time, until it's lightly golden, about 5 minutes
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the honey, sesame oil, and 1 tablespoon of the sesame seeds until well combined
  4. Add the sauce to the skillet with the chicken and stir to evenly coat. Lower the heat to medium-low and let it simmer until the sauce thickens and the chicken is fully cooked
  5. Finish by sprinkling the remaining sesame seeds on top
